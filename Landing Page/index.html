What is Dream Drive?

Dream Drive is a next-generation brain–computer interface gaming platform that turns your thoughts into motion.

  • Captures brainwave signals to control in-game movement.
  • Monitors heart rate, temperature, and body vitals in real-time.
  • Automatically pauses or exits if health metrics go abnormal.

How It Works

Neural Sync

Reads neural patterns (EEG signals) in real-time.

AI Translator

AI converts signals into motion and actions.

SafePlay System

Constant monitoring of vitals ensures safety.

Immersive Feedback

The game reacts instantly to your thoughts.
